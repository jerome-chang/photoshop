About

Innovation is the key to advance urban railway development in Asia-Pacific. The Asia-Pacific Rail Innovation Collaboration (APRIC) is a collaboration platform linking the group of experts in the railway industry.

  • APRIC stimulates the needs to innovate by proofing the benefits of adopting better innovative technologies.
  • APRIC advances the speed of technology landing through identification of applicable test-beds for prototyping.
  • APRIC gathers empirical data to improve the fitting of the solutions to the urban railway sector.
  • APRIC seeks performing and efficient integration of solutions from the planning, system design, construction, operating, maintenance, through to the stage of asset management.
  • APRIC connects experts in the urban railway sector within Asia-Pacific region to prepare the best-available solutions for the future.
  • APRIC fosters G2G (government-to-government), G2E (government-to-enterprise), and E2E (enterprise-to-enterprise) collaboration.

APRIC works in the bounds of research, testing, prototyping, technology transfer, and commercialization for innovative technologies that will make urban railway safer, more efficient to commuters and the environment, and offering more satisfying riding experience.

Message from Co-Chairs

Azmi Aziz

Welcome to the first edition of Asia Pacific Rail Innovation Collaboration (APRIC) news updates to all members of the public. It is an exciting time for all of us at APRIC to begin a new journey of creating a teamwork of enthusiastic professionals in the railway industry to venture into the dimension of innovations with the focus to help improve the efficiency of the industry that will benefit all stakeholders such as the railway operators, OEMs, research institutes, universities and the public at large.


We are equally excited of this journey because we believe this effort will be able to bring all levels of cooperation and collaborations of various parties to share each and everyone’s experiences to enhance the opportunities that lies within the railway industry. On this note, we wish to inform that one of APRIC focus areas would be the region of South East Asia where vast opportunities await the railway connectivity into China as part of the Pan Asia Railway network.


We hope to be able to provide sufficient opportunities to all interested parties to pledge their support by joining APRIC as a member at the corporate or individual level. We are sure of sharing this exciting ties with as many members as possible.

Morris Cheung

It is time to grasp the opportunities.


Traditionally, railway is an industry not in the forefront in adopting new technologies. This situation is changing. With the advancement of Internet of Things (IoT), 5G, sensor technologies, cloud computing, AI, big data, robotic, etc. the railway industry is gaining momentum and increasing interest in the adoption of new technologies in order to enhance safety and equipment reliability and increase cost efficiency. With the development of eco-system nurturing innovation and start-up, application of new technology in railway industry is blooming.


There is an urban boom which happens mainly in Asia Pacific. Roughly the current 4 billion people living in urban areas will reach 6 billion in 2050. In parallel to the urban boom is the rapid development of mass transit systems in the Asia-Pacific region.


We believe it is time to grasp this golden opportunity. The goal of APRIC is to gather participants in the industry and in the region in order to speed up the change for the benefits of travelling communities.
