Membership types

Welcome to the alliance of urban railway experts! There are 2 types of membership opened for application. Please complete the Membership Application Form either by filling in online or download from here.

ou will be required to share your Organization or Individual’s status about the following:

  • Your technology expertise
  • Innovations practiced
  • Geographical application of the technology
  • Attachment to institutions, in any
  • Stage of the innovations

Corporate Member

  • Represented by an organization, authority, enterprise, institution or academia;
  • Successfully completed the Membership Application Form and be approved by the APRIC Executive Committee;
  • Entitled to have 2 representatives, including a Primary Representative and an Alternate Representative;
  • Eligible to present technological innovations in concepts, prototypes, products or solutions at APRIC events;
  • Has full voting right at APRIC General Meetings.

Individual Member

  • Successfully completed the Membership Application Form and be approved by the APRIC Executive Committee;
  • Eligible to attend APRIC events;
  • Has full voting right at APRIC General Meetings.
