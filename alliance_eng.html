1st APRIC Rail Innova@on Awards, 2020

APRIC will proudly organize the 1st APRIC Rail Innovation Awards in 2020. APRIC is initiated as a platform to promote, advocate and recognize innovation in the railway industry. These Awards aim at:

  • To make technological innovations relating to the railway industry visible
  • To recognize those contributing to innovations
  • To announce and measure the benefits cultivated by such innovations
  • To help evaluate the sophistication of technological innovations relating to the railway sector
  • To foster collaboration in the Asia-Pacific region towards the landing of innovations
  • To construct a solid network in the advocacy of innovations

Target

The APRIC Rail Innovation Awards fit the following groups of participants:
  • Current APRIC members
  • Potential APRIC members
  • Railway policy and planners
  • Technology owners, enterprises and organizations
  • Railway technology adopters

Awards Entry and Nomination

Participation is by nomination only:
  • The proposers are opened to all, either APRIC members and non-members.
  • The nominees can be current APRIC members and non-members.
  • There are no limits on the number of nomination by any proposers.

Benefits for winners

The winning innovations receive the following recognition:
  • Receiving the highest-level recognition in the railway sector within Asia-Pacific
  • Announcement at APRIC website, and social media exposure
  • Lined-up interviews opportunities with local and regional media, trade media
  • Opportunities to showcase the winning innovations at APRIC events
  • Opportunities to present the winning innovations at APRIC members’ events
  • Entitlement to display the APRIC Awards’ logo on the winners’ printer matters, press materials, advertisements, and other promotional materials
