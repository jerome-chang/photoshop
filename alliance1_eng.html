APRIC Study Tours and Innovation Dialogues,2020

Location

Various, cities in Asia-Pacific that offers high potential of technological innovation adoption in the railway sector, including Beijing, Chengdu, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, and others.

Objectives

Study Tours and Innovation Dialogues are catered for APRIC members to exchange and present ideas and prototypes of technological innovations with transport and railway authorities, railway operators, scientists, engineers, policy planners, and advocates. Members are guaranteed to have very high-level exchanges and presentations with critical decision makers.
